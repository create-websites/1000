A SACRED TIMELINE

Bhumi Pujan

The foundational ritual to honor Mother Earth, unifying sacred soil and water from across India to sanctify the ground and begin construction.

JANUARY 22, 2024

Pran Pratishtha

The sacred consecration ceremony that infused the idol with divine life force, transforming the stone structure into a living abode of God.

THE PILGRIM SURGE

The temple's inauguration triggered an unprecedented wave of tourism, transforming Ayodhya into India's foremost spiritual destination almost overnight.

Visitor Growth: 2022 vs. 2024

This chart compares the total visitors for the entire year of 2022 against the numbers for just the first nine months of 2024, highlighting the exponential increase.

REIMAGINING A GLOBAL CITY

The temple has catalyzed a massive urban transformation, with over ₹85,000 Crore invested in the Ayodhya Master Plan to build a world-class spiritual metropolis.

Master Plan Investment Focus

A significant portion of the development fund is allocated to creating robust infrastructure and enhancing the pilgrim experience.
