The Conscious Coast

The future of travel is being written on coastlines pioneering a more sustainable approach. These destinations prove that a thriving tourism industry can support healthy ecosystems and empower local communities.

Case Study: Costa Rica

Costa Rica has built its national brand on environmental stewardship. The philosophy of "Pura Vida" (pure life) is reflected in its commitment to conservation, making it a global leader in ecotourism.

Over 25% of Costa Rica's landmass is protected in national parks and reserves.
