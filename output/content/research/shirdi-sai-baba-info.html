Return to Shirdi

After a mysterious absence, Baba returns permanently to Shirdi, taking up residence in a dilapidated mosque he names "Dwarkamai."

1908

Gurupurnima Begins

Baba gives permission for the first formal worship of him as a Guru, establishing the only festival he explicitly asked to be celebrated.

3

Mahasamadhi

On the auspicious day of Vijayadashami, Sai Baba consciously leaves his mortal body, leaving an eternal legacy of love and service.
