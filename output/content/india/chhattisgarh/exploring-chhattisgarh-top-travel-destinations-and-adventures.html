Destination Description Adventure Activities
Chitrakote Falls
Often referred to as the 'Niagara Falls of India', Chitrakote Falls is the broadest waterfall in the country and a major tourist attraction in Chhattisgarh. The waterfall is especially beautiful during the monsoon season when it is in full flow.
 Boating, Photography
Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary
Located in the northern part of Mahasamund district of Chhattisgarh, Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary is known for its lush green vegetation and unique wildlife. It is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including several rare and endangered species.
 Wildlife Safari, Bird Watching
Kanger Valley National Park
One of the most beautiful and densest national parks of India, Kanger Valley National Park is known for its biodiversity, stunning landscapes, and the famous Kutumsar Caves and Kailash Caves.
 Trekking, Cave Exploration, Wildlife Safari
Bhoramdeo Temple
Often referred to as the 'Khajuraho of Chhattisgarh', Bhoramdeo Temple is a complex of Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temples are known for their exquisite carvings and sculptures, and are a fine example of ancient Indian architecture.
 Heritage Walk, Photography
Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary
Located in the Bilaspur district of Chhattisgarh, Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary is a part of the Achanakmar-Amarkantak Biosphere Reserve. The sanctuary is home to a variety of wildlife, including tigers, leopards, and several species of deer.
 Wildlife Safari, Nature Walks
