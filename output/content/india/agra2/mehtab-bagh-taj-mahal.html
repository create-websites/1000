"Mehtab Bagh: Sunset Bliss by the Taj Mahal"

Mehtab Bagh, a serene riverside garden in Agra, offers stunning sunset views of the Taj Mahal, lush greenery, and a tranquil escape from city life. A historical gem from the Mughal era, it’s a must-visit destination for nature lovers, history enthusiasts, and photographers alike.
