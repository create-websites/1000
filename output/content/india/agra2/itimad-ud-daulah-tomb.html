"Discover Agra's Hidden Gem: The 'Baby Taj'"

The Tomb of Itimad-ud-Daulah, known as the 'Baby Taj,' is a masterpiece of Mughal architecture in Agra, showcasing intricate white marble inlay work and marking a pivotal shift in aesthetic innovation. Built by Empress Nur Jahan between 1622-1628 as a tribute to her father, the serene mausoleum offers historical significance and artistic splendor in a tranquil setting.
