"Explore Tripura: Palaces, Hills, and Wildlife Adventures Await!"

The article provides a guide to five must-visit destinations in Tripura, India, each offering unique experiences: Ujjayanta Palace, a historical museum in Agartala; Neermahal, a picturesque water palace; Unakoti, an ancient pilgrimage site with rock carvings; Jampui Hills, known for its breathtaking views and orange orchards; and Sipahijola Wildlife Sanctuary, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.


Destination Description Adventure Activities
Ujjayanta Palace
Located in the capital city of Agartala, Ujjayanta Palace is a former royal palace that now serves as a museum. It's a must-visit for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts.
 City tour, Museum exploration
Neermahal
Neermahal, or 'Water Palace', is a stunning architectural marvel situated in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake. It's the perfect spot for photography enthusiasts.
 Boating, Photography
Unakoti
Unakoti is an ancient pilgrimage site featuring rock carvings and murals. It's a unique destination that offers a glimpse into the region's rich cultural heritage.
 Trekking, Heritage walk
Jampui Hills
Jampui Hills is the highest range in Tripura, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes. It's also famous for its orange orchards.
 Hiking, Bird watching, Orange picking
Sipahijola Wildlife Sanctuary
This wildlife sanctuary is home to a variety of species, including clouded leopards and spectacled monkeys. It's a paradise for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.
 Wildlife spotting, Nature walks
