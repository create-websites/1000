Destination Description Adventure
Bhubaneswar
The capital city of Odisha, Bhubaneswar is known for its architecture and ancient temples. It is also a thriving centre for commerce and business.
 Temple exploration, city tours, shopping
Puri
Puri is known for its spiritual significance, beaches and the famous Jagannath Temple. It is one of the original Char Dham pilgrimage sites for Hindus.
 Beach activities, temple visit, Rath Yatra
Konark
Home to the world heritage site, the Sun Temple, Konark is also known for its artisanal stone carving activities.
 Heritage walks, beach activities
Chilika Lake
Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, Chilika Lake is a paradise for bird watchers and nature lovers. It's also home to the endangered Irrawaddy Dolphins.
 Bird watching, boating, dolphin spotting
Simlipal National Park
One of the largest national parks in India, Simlipal is a tiger reserve, an elephant reserve, and a biosphere reserve in Odisha. It's known for its stunning waterfalls, rich flora and fauna.
 Wildlife safari, trekking, bird watching
