Destination Description Adventure
Dijon
Known as the capital of the Burgundy region, Dijon is famous for its mustard, wine, and historical architecture. The city is home to the Palace of the Dukes, where the Dukes of Burgundy once lived.
 Explore the city's history by visiting the Palace of the Dukes, and don't forget to try the famous Dijon mustard.
Beaune
Beaune is the wine capital of Burgundy and is known for its annual wine auction. The city is also home to the Hospices de Beaune, a former charitable almshouse.
 Take a tour of the vineyards, participate in a wine tasting session, and visit the Hospices de Beaune.
Besançon
Besançon is a beautiful city located in the Doubs department, surrounded by hills and a loop of the Doubs River. The city is known for its Citadel, an impressive fortress offering panoramic views.
 Visit the Citadel, explore the city's museums, and enjoy a boat ride on the Doubs River.
Jura Mountains
The Jura Mountains offer stunning natural beauty with its lakes, waterfalls, and forests. It's a perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts.
 Enjoy hiking, skiing, and wildlife spotting in the Jura Mountains.
Chalon-sur-Saône
Chalon-sur-Saône is a city on the Saône River known for its art and history. It's also the birthplace of photography and hosts a street art festival every July.
 Visit the Nicéphore Niépce Museum, explore the city's art galleries, and attend the street art festival.
