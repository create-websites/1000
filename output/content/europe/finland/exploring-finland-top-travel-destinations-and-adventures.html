Destination Description Adventure
Helsinki
Helsinki, the capital of Finland, is a vibrant seaside city of beautiful islands and great green parks. The city’s rhythm is laid back yet at the same time refreshingly active in terms of both the number and quality of restaurants and nightclubs.
 City tours, Museum visits, Nightlife exploration
Lapland
Lapland is Finland’s northernmost region, a sparsely populated area bordering Sweden, Norway, Russia and the Baltic Sea. It is known for its vast subarctic wilderness, ski resorts and natural phenomena including the midnight sun and the Northern Lights.
 Skiing, Northern Lights viewing, Reindeer sledding
Turku
Turku, the oldest city in Finland, is rich in history and culture. It is home to a stunning archipelago, medieval architecture, and a bustling food scene. The city is also known for its music festivals and vibrant nightlife.
 Historical tours, Food tasting, Music festivals
Savonlinna
Savonlinna is a city in southeast Finland, in the heart of the Saimaa lake region. The city offers beautiful landscapes, a famous opera festival, and the Olavinlinna Castle, a stunning medieval fortress that appears to float on the water.
 Opera festival, Castle visit, Lake tours
Rovaniemi
Rovaniemi is the capital of Lapland, in northern Finland. It's known as a gateway to the Arctic, and is the official hometown of Santa Claus. The city offers a variety of winter activities, from snowmobiling to ice fishing.
 Santa Claus Village visit, Snowmobiling, Ice fishing
