"Explore Western Australia: From Perth's Skyline to Karijini's Wilderness"

The article provides a comprehensive guide to five must-visit destinations in Western Australia, including Perth, Rottnest Island, Margaret River, Broome, and Karijini National Park. Each location offers unique attractions, from city tours and beach activities in Perth, to snorkeling and quokka selfies on Rottnest Island, wine tasting and surfing in Margaret River, camel riding and pearl shopping in Broome, and hiking and photography in Karijini National Park.


Destination Description Adventure
Perth
Perth, the capital city of Western Australia, is a vibrant city with a mix of modern architecture and natural beauty. It's known for its stunning beaches, expansive parks, and impressive skyline.
 City tours, beach activities, and exploring Kings Park
Rottnest Island
Rottnest Island is a popular destination for its beautiful beaches, crystal clear waters, and unique wildlife, including the adorable quokkas.
 Snorkeling, cycling, and quokka selfies
Margaret River
Margaret River is a small town south of Perth, known for its craft breweries, boutiques and surrounding wineries. It's also famous for its strong surf, whale watching opportunities, and show caves.
 Wine tasting, surfing, and caving

Karijini National Park is a vast wilderness area in the Hamersley Range of Western Australia. It's known for its dramatic creeks, spectacular gorges, and beautiful waterfalls.
 Hiking, swimming, and photography
